Cabinet wisdom

Tips from the arcade · old-school survival

Asteroids: don't hyperspace early

Hyperspace is your panic button — but it places you randomly, sometimes inside a rock. Save it for wave 6+ when the screen is too crowded to thrust safely.

  • Big rock 20 pts
  • Medium rock 50 pts
  • Small rock 100 pts

Lunar Lander: cut thrust late

Gravity is gentle but compounds. Thrust hard early to kill horizontal velocity, then coast and feather the engine in the last 50 meters. Fuel matters; don't overshoot.

  • Soft landing ×2 fuel bonus
  • Pad bullseye +500
  • Out of fuel game over

Simon: chunk your patterns

Don't memorize note-by-note past round 8. Group sequences in chunks of 3–4 and rehearse the rhythm aloud. The brain holds melody longer than digits.

  • Each repeat +1 pt
  • Round 20+ 2× speed
  • Round 30+ random tempo
