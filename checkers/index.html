How to play

You play red, the computer plays black. Click a piece to see legal moves; click a destination to move. The Captures selector switches between mandatory jumps (tournament rule) and optional jumps (casual play).

Reach the opposite back row to crown a man into a king; kings move both forward and backward diagonally. Win by capturing all enemy pieces or leaving them with no legal moves.

  • Easy — depth 2 with extra randomness; will miss tactics.
  • Medium — depth 5.
  • Hard — depth 7. Sees several moves ahead; trades fairly.
