Information we collect

Limestone Games does not require user registration or collect personal information directly. We may collect non-personally identifiable information automatically, including browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent on pages through analytics and advertising services.

Cookies & local storage

We use localStorage to save your game preferences (such as high scores and display settings). Most of this data stays on your device and is never transmitted.

Casino stats cloud backup. To let your casino stats survive a browser cache wipe, we mirror them to a small storage record on Cloudflare. The record is keyed by a random per-device ID generated in your browser; we do not collect or store your name, email, or any other personal information for this backup. The backup contains only your in-game stats (bankroll, hand counts, peak amounts, rare-event counters) and an optional display name you chose locally. Anyone who knows your random device ID can read or modify that backup, so treat it like any other browser-stored secret. Clearing your browser data ends your access to the backup; a future "Disconnect" button will let you delete it explicitly.

Third-party ad servers or ad networks may use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or web beacons in their advertisements and links that appear on Limestone Games.
