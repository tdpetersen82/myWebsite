Player notes

Opening moves · pattern recognition · winning lines

Connect 4: own the center

The middle column passes through 7 of the 21 winning lines — more than any other column. Open with column 4 and the AI on hard mode loses about 60% as second player.

  • Center first +30% win
  • Double threat guaranteed
  • Odd rows for first player strong

Connect 4: count squares ahead

Every move you can immediately win on the next turn is a "threat." Two threats at once = forced win. Hint mode highlights the first threat — counting the second is on you.

  • One threat blockable
  • Two threats winning
  • Three threats show off

Dots & Boxes: chain control

Late game is all about who gets stuck closing chains. Sacrifice a short chain to force your opponent to open a long one. Look for "hard-hearted handouts" — give two boxes to claim five.

  • Short chain (≤2) sacrifice
  • Long chain (3+) steal
  • Double-cross +net 2
