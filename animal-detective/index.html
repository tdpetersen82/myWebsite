How to play Animal Detective

Animal Detective is a friendly twenty-questions game for kids. Zookeeper Maddie picks a mystery animal from her menagerie of 76 creatures, and your job is to figure out which one. You ask yes-or-no questions from four categories — Body & Size, Habitat, Food & Behavior, and People — and use the clues to narrow the suspects down. When you're confident, tap "I think I know!" and pick the animal you suspect.

Controls

  • Tap a category tab to switch question pools (Body, Habitat, Food, People)
  • Tap any question to ask it — Maddie answers yes or no
  • Tap I think I know! to open the animal grid and lock in your guess
  • Tap the Hint button (one per round) to eliminate roughly half of the remaining suspects

Tips & strategy

  • Start broad. "Is it a mammal?" or "Is it bigger than a person?" cuts the field in half on the first question.
  • Then narrow by habitat. Land, water, air, or extreme — habitat questions slice the remaining group fast.
  • Save the hint for late. The hint is most valuable when you're stuck between 6 or 8 finalists, not when there are 70 suspects left.
  • Eliminate, don't confirm. "Does it have feathers?" rules out every mammal at once. Each yes/no answer should kill a category, not pick one animal.
  • Don't waste questions on close cousins. If you've narrowed it to two cats, just guess. You only get 20 questions per round.

About Animal Detective

Animal Detective is built on the classic twenty-questions game, scoped for ages 4 to 10 with friendly art and clear yes/no logic. The 76 animals range from familiar pets and farm animals to zoo favorites and a handful of obscure creatures — chosen to give kids real "I didn't know that!" moments. The Zookeeper Maddie character is the narrator and answer-giver throughout.

Frequently asked

How do I play Animal Detective? Tap yes-or-no questions to gather clues about Maddie's mystery animal, then guess from the animal grid when you're confident.

Is Animal Detective free? Yes — no signup, no ads on the gameplay, no downloads.

How many animals? 76 creatures across mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, fish, and a few invertebrates.

What's the question limit? Up to 20 questions per case, plus one hint that eliminates roughly half the remaining suspects.
