# Dirt Bike Hill Climb — QA Checklist (150 Questions)

## Loading & Menu
1. Does the loading bar appear and fill up when you first open the game?
2. Does the menu screen appear after loading completes?
3. Is the game title displayed clearly on the menu?
4. Can you see the animated bike preview on the menu screen?
5. Are the bike's wheels spinning in the menu preview?
6. Are the control instructions visible and readable on the menu?
7. Does the "Press ENTER or tap to start" text blink?
8. Does pressing ENTER start the game from the menu?
9. Does pressing SPACE start the game from the menu?
10. Does tapping the screen start the game on mobile?
11. If you have a previous high score, does it show on the menu?
12. If you've never played before, is the high score hidden or zero?

## Basic Movement
13. Does the bike move forward when you press UP/W?
14. Does the bike stop accelerating when you release the gas?
15. Does the bike slow down when you press DOWN/S (brake)?
16. Does the bike lean backward when you press LEFT/A?
17. Does the bike lean forward when you press RIGHT/D?
18. Do arrow keys work the same as WASD?
19. Does the bike feel like it has weight and momentum?
20. Does the bike coast when you let go of all keys on flat ground?
21. Does the speed display update as you accelerate?
22. Does the speed display drop when you brake?
23. Can you bring the bike to a near-complete stop with the brake?

## Physics & Gravity
24. Does gravity pull the bike downward when airborne?
25. Does the bike land naturally after going off a hill?
26. Does the gravity feel appropriate — not floaty, not too heavy?
27. Does the bike bounce slightly on hard landings?
28. Does the bike behave differently going uphill vs downhill?
29. Does the bike pick up speed going downhill?
30. Does the bike slow down going uphill?
31. Does momentum carry you over small bumps?
32. Does the bike feel stable on flat terrain?
33. If you stop on a slope, does the bike slide or roll?

## Suspension & Wheels
