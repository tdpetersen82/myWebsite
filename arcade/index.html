Cabinet wisdom

3 quick reads

Block Puzzle: keep the right column open

Tetrominoes only clear when a row is full. Save your rightmost column for I-pieces and you'll get four-line clears (Tetrises) consistently.

  • Single line 100 pts
  • Tetris (4 lines) 800 pts
  • Soft drop +1 per cell

Breakout: aim for the corners

The ball bounces sharper off paddle edges. Clip the side of the paddle to angle the ball into untouched columns and you'll dismantle the wall faster.

  • Top row brick 50 pts
  • Bottom row brick 10 pts
  • All cleared +1,000

Asteroids: thrust in pulses

Continuous thrust kills you. Tap thrust to drift, point and fire — the inertia of small rocks is your best friend.

  • Large rock 20 pts
  • Medium rock 50 pts
  • Small rock 100 pts
