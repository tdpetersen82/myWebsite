House guide

Quick rules · strategy notes · honest odds

When to hit, when to hold

Basic strategy reduces the house edge to ~0.5%. Hit on 16 vs. dealer 7+, stand on 17+. Always split aces and eights.

  • Soft 17 Hit
  • Pair of 8s Split
  • 11 vs 6 Double

Outside bets pay less, win more

Red/black or odd/even pays 1:1 with ~48.6% odds. Straight up on a single number pays 35:1 — the house keeps that gap.

  • Red/Black 1:1
  • Dozen 2:1
  • Straight 35:1

Hold the draw, chase the royal

Jacks or Better rewards discipline: keep any high pair, but break it for four to a royal. Optimal holds keep the house edge near 0.46% — the best odds on the floor.

  • High pair Hold
  • 4 to a royal Break pair
  • Royal flush 800:1
