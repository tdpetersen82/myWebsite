How to play

The board is a 3×3 grid of nine smaller tic-tac-toe boards. You play X, the AI plays O. X moves first and can pick any cell on the board.

After your move, the position you played (top-left, center, bottom-right, etc.) tells your opponent which mini-board they must play in next. If that mini-board is already won or full, the opponent gets a free choice anywhere on the board.

Win a mini-board by getting three in a row inside it — that claims the matching cell on the meta board. Win three mini-boards in a row on the meta board to win the game.

  • Easy — depth 2 with extra randomness; misses tactics.
  • Medium — depth 4 with positional weighting and threat detection.
  • Hard — depth 6 with meta-aware scoring; plays for long-term board control.
