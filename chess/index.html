Click a piece, then click where to move it. Green dots show legal moves; rings show captures. The AI plays the other color — switch sides any time.

How to play

Click a piece, then click a destination. Legal moves are highlighted; illegal moves are simply ignored. The AI moves automatically after you.

All standard rules are enforced: castling on both sides, en passant, pawn promotion (with a piece picker), check / checkmate / stalemate, plus 50-move and threefold-repetition draws.

  • Easy — depth 2, jittered move choice. Great for learning the rules.
  • Medium — depth 3. Plays solidly; a strong club player will still beat it.
  • Hard — depth 4 + quiescence search on captures. Targets ~1200–1500 club strength.

Your wins, losses, and best streak save locally. Switching difficulty mid-game is fine; the AI just uses the new tier on its next move.
