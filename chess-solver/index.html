CHESS AI

Minimax solver for cardgames.io/chess

How to Use

  1. Go to cardgames.io/chess and start a game against any opponent.
  2. Drag the bookmarklet below to your bookmarks bar, then click it on the chess page.
    Or: Open DevTools (F12), go to Console, paste the script from solver.js, and press Enter.
  3. A floating Chess AI panel appears. Click AI Move to play the best move, or toggle Auto-Play to let it run.
  4. Adjust the Depth slider (1-5) to trade speed for strength. Depth 3-4 is recommended.

Chess AI

Features

Minimax + Alpha-Beta Searches ahead with pruning for efficient play up to depth 5.
Piece-Square Tables Positional evaluation encourages strong piece placement.
Move Ordering Captures, checks, and promotions searched first for faster cutoffs.
Auto-Play Mode Watches for your turn and plays automatically.
