About Limestone Games

Limestone Games is a small, independent collection of browser games — built and maintained by one person, not a studio. The idea is simple: a tidy shelf of classics you can open and play in two seconds, with no account, no app to install, and nothing nagging you to sign up.

What you'll find here

Dozens of games across four shelves: Arcade (Snake, Breakout, Asteroids, Space Invaders and other coin-op classics), Kids (gentle games for ages 4–10 with no timers or fail states), Strategy (Chess, Checkers, Othello, Backgammon and more against a built-in AI), and a play-money Casino (Blackjack, Roulette, Video Poker, Solitaire and friends). Everything is rebuilt to load fast and run on phones, tablets, and desktops.

No accounts, no nonsense

You never have to register. Your high scores and preferences are saved in your own browser via localStorage, so they stay on your device. The only thing that ever leaves your browser is an optional, anonymous cloud backup of your casino stats — keyed by a random ID, with no name or email attached. See the Privacy Policy for the details.

Play money only

The casino games are for fun and practice only. They use virtual chips with no monetary value — there is no real-money gambling, no purchases, no deposits, and no way to cash out. They're a place to learn the odds and strategy of the games without risking a cent.
