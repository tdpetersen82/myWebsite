How to play

You play Black, the computer plays White. Each move must place a disc so it sandwiches one or more of your opponent's discs in a straight line — horizontal, vertical, or diagonal. Every sandwiched disc flips to your color.

If you have no legal move, your turn passes automatically. When neither side can move (or the board fills up), the side with more discs wins.

  • Easy — depth 2 with extra randomness; will overlook tactics.
  • Medium — depth 4 with positional weighting.
  • Hard — depth 6 with mobility + corner play. Switches to a perfect endgame solver in the final 12 empty squares.
