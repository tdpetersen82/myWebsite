How to play

The board has 12 small pits — six per side — and one large store on each end. You play the bottom row; the AI plays the top. Your store is on the right, the AI's on the left. The game starts with 4 seeds in every small pit.

On your turn, pick one of your six pits. Take all its seeds and drop one into each pit going counter-clockwise (rightward on your row, then up to the AI's row going left), skipping the AI's store. Land the last seed in your own store → take another turn. Land the last seed in one of your own previously-empty pits → capture all seeds in the opposite pit (plus your seed) into your store.

The game ends as soon as one side has no seeds in any of its six pits. The other player sweeps any remaining seeds on their side into their store. Most seeds in store wins.

  • Easy — depth 2 with randomness; misses most captures.
  • Medium — depth 6 with capture-aware scoring.
  • Hard — depth 10 with mobility and chain-turn heuristics.
