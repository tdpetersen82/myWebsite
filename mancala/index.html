How to play

The board has 12 small pits — six per side — and one large store on each end. You play the bottom row; the computer plays the top. Your store is on the right, the computer's on the left. The game starts with 4 seeds in every small pit.

On your turn, pick one of your six pits. Take all its seeds and drop one into each pit going counter-clockwise (rightward on your row, then up to the computer's row going left), skipping the computer's store. Land the last seed in your own store → take another turn. Land the last seed in one of your own previously-empty pits → capture all seeds in the opposite pit (plus your seed) into your store.

The game ends as soon as one side has no seeds in any of its six pits. The other player sweeps any remaining seeds on their side into their store. Most seeds in store wins.

  • Preview before you commit — hover a pit on a mouse, or tap once to preview and again to play it on touch.
  • Hint — stuck? The Hint button highlights a strong move for you.
  • Undo — take back your last move (and the computer's reply) while a game is in progress.
  • Two looks — switch between the warm Heirloom Hardwood board and the dark Twilight Slate board any time; your choice is remembered.
  • Easy — depth 2 with randomness; misses most captures. Medium — depth 6, capture-aware. Hard — depth 10 with mobility and chain-turn heuristics.
