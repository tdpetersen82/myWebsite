How to play Bubble Pop

Bubble Pop is a friendly tap game for kids. Bright bubbles float up from the bottom of the screen, and your job is to pop every one before it drifts off the top. Five misses ends the round — but the bubbles get faster and more colorful as you level up.

Controls

  • Tap or click — pop a bubble
  • Restart — start a new round

Tips & strategy

  • Watch the slowest bubbles. Each bubble drifts at its own speed. The ones moving the slowest are the easy points — grab them first.
  • Don't panic-tap. Wild taps in empty space count as misses. Aim before you tap.
  • Catch the edge bubbles. Bubbles near the side walls bounce back — chase them as they cross the screen.
  • Steady wins. Every level the bubbles speed up. Keep a calm rhythm rather than racing the screen.

About Bubble Pop

Bubble Pop is the modern web version of the tap-the-target game format that's been a kids-app staple since the early iPad days. The mechanic is simple: pop bubbles, watch your score, beat your best. No accounts, no in-app purchases.
